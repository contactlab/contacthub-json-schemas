Welcome to Contacthub Developer Reference


Every day, our customers connect with us in countless ways, resulting in interactions that often translate into an ever-growing and widely distributed patchwork of data. What if we could simply transform all of this data into knowledge? Enhanced user profiles that are up-to-date, easy to use and access, and always available in one place. Contacthub is the perfect solution, enabling you to collect, link, store and update data in real-time while making it readily available to your own platforms, to match your individual needs.

How? The powerful REST API, SDK, JavaScript Analytics and third party software plug-ins enable simple and effective integration with your systems, providing a comprehensive picture of your customers in a single place, while ensuring that it changes with each new interaction.

The plug-ins have been specifically developed to allow integration of, for example, e-commerce, CRM and similar third party software.

Here you can find all the materials that you need to help you build your own engagement solution, using the impressive features of Contacthub.
